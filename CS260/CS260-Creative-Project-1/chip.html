Chip Cookies

Bakery
159 500 N, Provo, UT 84601
chipcookies.co

Recommended Order:

  • A box of "OG" chocolate chip cookies with milk

Reviews:

  • Elsie W.

    If I could I would give them a 3.5. But I decided to round up. I've held out for so long because I am a devout levian fan...but in my opinion you can't even compare the two cookies. The only thing that is the same is probably the size.
    Chip is awesome! I do really love that they deliver. I love that they have a specialty cookie of the month and that it changes.
    I will say I knocked them stars because as much as I love a gooey center, I don't love a literally not cooked in the middle cookie. I had to throw my cookies back in the oven at home because the middle of the cookies were totally sagging when I picked them up. Those are little too underdone imo.
    MY ONLY WISH - semi sweet chocolate chips.

  • Shannon B.

    We love out of state but every time we come to town we pick up a box (or 2) of chip cookies. They are SO delicious! We're had big cookies from other companies and they simply don't compare. Chip is #1

  • Kelsey C.

    Eh. It's a cookie. For me personally it was way too greasy. I sat a cookie on my pants while I was eating it because let's face it, they're huge and so sweet that the average person can't eat more than maybe two bites at a time. The cookie left a round grease stain on my pants :(
    Because of how sweet they are, and how oily they are, after you eat a couple bites you feel lethargic. I don't hate them, but it's just a little much for even a sugar lover like myself so that's saying something.
