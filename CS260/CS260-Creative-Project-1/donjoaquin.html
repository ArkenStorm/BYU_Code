Reviews:

  • Rachel B.

    Honest to goodness best Authentic Mexican food in Provo and the whole Utah valley. Very inexpensive as well! Excellent service and the food is ALWAYS very fresh!

  • Kevin W.

    Money. Everything was awesome. Meats, salsas, price. I'd go back for sure. Friendly service.

    Favorites were the pollo and al pastor. Pineapple salsa was too spicy for my wife, but perfect for me.

  • Andres A.

    Oh Don Juaquin, one of the best street tacos in Utah County, great taste and variety of meets, and great environment, music and location! I have come here for years whenever I'm in need of a good taco! I would totally leave this place a five star review, however recently, their tacos have not been what they used to be, they are not as juicy or flavorful, still amazing but not living up to the Don Juaquin name. Understandable since they have gotten so busy, with lines out the door, that they aren't able to prepare the best tacos. Still, amazing tacos!

  • Karlie G.

    Don Joaquin is love, Don Joaquin is life. La Gringa is one of my favorite things to get there, it's my go-to after a hard day.

  • Nadia P.

    This is a great place to go with friends, plus tacos are always amazing. #TacoTuesdays!
